5 step skincare routine

STEP ONE: CLEANSE

  • What: Removes makeup & impurities from face.
  • How: Apply to a wet face & gently apply to face. Then rinse. Repeat as necessary (i.e heavy makeup).

STEP TWO: TONE

  • What: Depending on type of toner it adds benefits ie. reduce pores or add hydration.
  • How: Apply with hands or a cotton pad gently pat on face & neck.

STEP THREE: EXFOLIATE

  • What: Gently unclog pores, smooth wrinkles & even skin tone.
  • How: Apply with hands or a cotton pad gently pat on face & neck.

STEP FOUR: TREAT

  • What: Depends on skin needs ie. smaller pores, prevent breakouts ect.
  • How: Apply with hands by using small amount to target areas on face or whole face.

STEP FIVE: MOISTURISE

  • What: Hydrates skin & helps retain moisture.
  • How: Apply a liberal amount to face & neck.
  • *Don't forget to use a sunscreen daily, especially if your using an exfoliant or other active ingridents.
